SEESOUND is a premium Vietnamese coffee company based in Toronto, Canada. They offer sustainably grown and ethically sourced beans from family-owned farms in Buôn Mê Thuột, Dak Lak.

Recognizing the need to stand out in a competitive market, we aimed to create a unique brand identity that highlights the coffee’s premium quality and connects with coffee enthusiasts worldwide.

syn·es·the·sia

noun
Experiencing some things in a different way from most other people, for example experiencing colours as sounds, experiencing shapes as tastes, or feeling something in one part of the body when a different part is stimulated.

The name SEESOUND is a play off the concept of synesthesia, a bizarre overlap of sensations.

We highlighted this concept by creating multi-sensory visuals that take the audience on a journey from the serene naturescapes of Dak Lak to the electrifying streets of Saigon.

The scope included creating visually striking packaging that would standout at events and on shelves of retail stores.

Digital Marketing

Along with packaging design and brand identity, SEESOUND needed to grow it’s presence online through digital marketing. We approached their marketing assets with a playful tone to reflect the zen but zany personality of the brand.
